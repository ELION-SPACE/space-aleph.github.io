ELION-SPACE

Software-first mission systems for future space platforms.

ELION-SPACE is the space-focused research surface of the Elion ecosystem. The current public work is early-stage and centered on mission autonomy, simulation-grounded software architecture, and bounded decision logic for fragile or high-value payloads.

Current Scope

  • Mission-level autonomy research
  • Simulation support for early mission concepts
  • Software-first architecture for future space systems
  • Explainable action gating and operational constraints

Current Boundaries

  • No flight-qualified software
  • No hardware validation
  • No spacecraft manufacturing claims
  • No pretense of operational mission readiness
